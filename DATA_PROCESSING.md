# Data Processing Workflow

The data-processing workflow includes:

1. standardization of country names;
2. standardization of year variables;
3. removal of aggregate or non-country records where applicable;
4. numeric plausibility checks;
5. duplicate country-year screening;
6. sequential merging using country-year keys;
7. construction of derived indicators;
8. export of processed analytical datasets.

Derived indicators include:

- Public_Exposure;
- Fiscal Pressure Index;
- Worker_Ratio;
- Affordability.
